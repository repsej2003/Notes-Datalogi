# Congrunce
for any $a,b\in \mathbb{Z}$ and $m\in \mathbb{Z}^+$
$$
a \equiv b (\text{mod } m) \Leftrightarrow m|(a-b)
$$
a is conguent to b modulo m

det samme som 
$$
\begin{align}
a \text{ mod m} = b \text{ mod m} \\
 \\
\exists  k\in \mathbb{Z} : a = b + km
\end{align}
$$
congruence is an equivalnce relaiton
